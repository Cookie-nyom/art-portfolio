My Art

What I create & why

I think my style is pretty easy to recognize, though it has changed a lot throughout the years and I do experiments sometimes. I haven't paid much attention to drawing challenges and collabs, but I'm open to them. I have enjoyed my time greatly connecting with others.

Right now I draw a lot of fanart for others. But normally I also draw OC's for others and myself. I used to draw a whole manga story too, though it wasn't professional but just some hobby as a 15-20 year old.

It’s mostly digitally. I will show some traditional art but those aren’t for commissions but just examples of my art journey with different mediums.
